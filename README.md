# 🏦 ATM Cash Demand Forecasting & Intelligent Decision System

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an intelligent data-driven system designed to analyze, predict, and optimize ATM cash demand. It focuses on transforming raw transactional and contextual ATM data into meaningful insights that support efficient cash management and operational decision-making.

The system integrates advanced data analysis, feature engineering, pattern recognition, and predictive intelligence to understand how different factors influence ATM usage. It helps identify demand trends, detect unusual behavior, and forecast future cash requirements.

---

## 🎯 Project Objective

The primary objective of this project is to build a smart analytical system that:

* Understands ATM usage patterns over time
* Identifies factors affecting cash withdrawals and deposits
* Groups ATMs based on demand behavior
* Detects unusual or abnormal transaction patterns
* Predicts future cash demand accurately
* Supports better decision-making for cash replenishment

---

## 📊 Data Understanding

The system uses structured ATM transaction data that includes:

* Daily withdrawal and deposit amounts
* Time-related information such as day and period
* Location characteristics (urban, rural, etc.)
* External influences such as holidays, events, and weather
* Competitive factors like nearby ATMs

This combination of transactional and contextual data allows deeper analysis of demand behavior.

---

## ⚙️ Feature Engineering (Core Intelligence of the Project)

Feature engineering plays a crucial role in enhancing the predictive and analytical power of the system. Each feature is designed to capture specific patterns or behaviors:

### 🔹 Time-Based Features

* Weekend indicators help identify increased activity during non-working days
* Monthly and quarterly indicators capture salary cycles and seasonal effects
* Month start and end indicators detect spending patterns linked to income and expenses

### 🔹 Trend and Rolling Features

* Rolling averages smooth short-term fluctuations and reveal consistent trends
* Growth rates show how demand is increasing or decreasing over time
* Trend slope identifies whether ATM usage is rising or falling

### 🔹 Behavioral Features

* Ratios between withdrawals and deposits highlight cash flow dynamics
* Utilization rate shows how efficiently ATM cash is used
* Demand-to-cash ratio helps detect potential shortages

### 🔹 Event-Based Features

* Holiday and event impact scores measure how special occasions affect demand
* Time gap features (since last event or until next event) help understand delayed or upcoming effects

### 🔹 Location-Based Features

* Competitor density captures the effect of nearby ATMs on usage
* Urban demand index reflects higher activity in densely populated areas
* Risk scores identify ATMs prone to overuse or underuse

### 🔹 Historical (Lag) Features

* Previous day and previous week data help capture repeating patterns
* These features improve prediction accuracy by incorporating past behavior

### 🔹 Statistical Features

* Volatility measures consistency or variability in demand
* Standardized values help detect extreme behavior

## 🔍 Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

EDA is used to explore and understand the dataset visually and statistically. The analysis focuses on:

* Distribution of withdrawals and deposits
* Time-based trends and seasonal variations
* Differences between weekdays and weekends
* Impact of holidays and special events
* Influence of external factors such as weather and competition

Insights derived from EDA help in identifying patterns, trends, and irregularities in ATM usage.

---

## 🧠 Clustering Analysis

Clustering is used to group ATMs based on similar demand behavior.

### Key Outcomes:

* Identification of high-demand ATMs with frequent usage
* Detection of moderate-demand ATMs with stable activity
* Recognition of low-demand ATMs with minimal usage

This categorization helps in planning cash allocation more effectively and understanding usage patterns across locations.

---

## ⚠️ Anomaly Detection

The system identifies unusual or unexpected patterns in ATM transactions.

### Key Focus:

* Sudden spikes in withdrawals during holidays or events
* Unexpected drops in activity
* Outliers that deviate significantly from normal behavior

Anomalies are important signals that may indicate special situations, risks, or operational issues.

---

## 🔮 Demand Prediction

The system forecasts future ATM cash demand based on historical data and engineered features.

### Key Capabilities:

* Predict next-day cash requirements
* Capture patterns influenced by time, location, and events
* Improve accuracy using past trends and behavioral features

This predictive capability enables proactive planning and avoids both shortages and excess cash.

---

## 💡 Decision Intelligence

The project goes beyond analysis and provides actionable insights:

* Identifies ATMs at risk of running out of cash
* Detects overstocked ATMs with unused cash
* Suggests optimal cash replenishment levels
* Generates alerts for unusual or high-risk situations

This transforms the system into a decision-support tool rather than just an analytical model.
